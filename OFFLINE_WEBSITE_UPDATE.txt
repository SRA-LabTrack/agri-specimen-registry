AGRISPECIMEN OFFLINE WEBSITE UPDATE
===================================

This update adds browser-based offline operation to the AgriSpecimen website.
It does not create a separate Android or iOS application.

WHAT WORKS OFFLINE AFTER ONE SUCCESSFUL ONLINE SIGN-IN
- Reopen the website and browse the cached registry.
- Search and filter all cached specimen fields.
- Open cached specimen details without opening every entry beforehand.
- View photographs that were automatically prepared in the browser cache.
- Add a specimen manually.
- Edit every field of an existing specimen.
- Add, replace, or remove compressed photographs.
- Change specimen status.
- Delete a specimen.
- Queue changes and synchronize them automatically when internet returns.

ONLINE-ONLY ACTION
- Excel bulk import remains online-only to avoid silently queuing hundreds of writes.

ECONOMY / SAFETY LIMITS
- Up to 500 registry rows are cached per signed-in account.
- Up to 150 specimen photo previews are prepared automatically.
- Remote photo cache is trimmed around 75 MB.
- Offline queue is limited to 40 changes and 24 MB of compressed queued photos.
- Deleted records also delete their stored Appwrite photos after synchronization.
